Hey People Of Bloc

I made this page as part of my application for the Junior Designer Position!

Iron Yard, Hack Reactor, Apprentice.io, General Assembly, Center Centre, and even Bloc have all been considered at some point. But this position seems like the best of both worlds.

There were a number of things that stood out to me in the posting. The fact that you all are a team of generalists and that designers own their projects from start to finish is extremely important to me, as that's how I've always worked. But one sentence stood out well above the rest:
