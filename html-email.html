Tides of Change

When I started at Torque Creative, our HTML emails were designed and developed for desktop only. Eventually, I noticed concerning (but not surprising) trends in our analytics. The number of views on mobile were increasing significantly!

Through a boat-load of research, I created a relatively simple responsive framework. While it proved to not be robust enough, it was easy to learn and helped both the clients and our team understand the difficulty and benefits of responsive email design.

I've since been using a modified framework of Mailchimp's Blueprints (specifically the modular template patterns) to lead in redesigns for a vast majority of Torque's clients.
