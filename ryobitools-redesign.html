Kick-Off

Torque's client, RYOBI Tools, came to us to redesign their website last summer. Having recently lost a creative director, our small team had to put together a solid presentation to prove we wanted to (and could) take on the project. We thought some visual aid, based heavily on user research & analytics, would help to prove that the project would be in good hands.

Torque was trusted with the project thanks to the hard work of a small team. Unfortunately, I was unable to contribute more to the design of this project. However, many elements found in the mockups were used by the lead designer. Score!
