Hey People Of Stripe

I made this page as part of my application for the Designer Position!

Unfortunately I wasn't sure who to address, but whoever you are, thank you for taking the time to read this and consider me. I found out about the position through the dynamics.js demo page and knew instantly I had to give it a shot. Stripe's branding is superb! The trust and quality being conveyed through all of the design gives me a strong feeling that those values are truly at the core of Stripe's culture.

I've thought of myself as a (junior) full-stack designer for some time, shortly after "full-stack developer" really caught on. There isn't a single aspect of design I don't want to learn more of, and I believe that you must have a solid understanding of a medium before you can design for it. I've done everything from lettering to making changes to front-end code in rails environments, and I'd love the opportunity to continue getting my hands dirty at Stripe.

Enough type. I hope my work can speak for itself.
