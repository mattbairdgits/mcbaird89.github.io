Hey Vulcun

I gave myself a three-(half)day challenge to give a taste of the kind of UX & UI skills I can share with the Vulcun team. I hope you enjoy the case study!

My initial thoughts for a design challenge included a landing page that announced Smite being added to Vulcun (because <3), but I decided to use the actual app before coming to a decision. I kept track of ideas and any pain points I noticed along the signup process.

After watching the video explaining the new features, I noticed an incredibly active chat room. UX insight gold mine! I interviewed a few users to see if they had any personal pain points with the app. Two users mentioned the mobile experience as having a few pain points, and when I had them elaborate, they both brought up the team building and editing features. I knew then and there that I had my challenge!

Challenge Accepted

After using the app for a few hours and some quick sketching & note-taking, I had enough material to open up Sketch and start fleshing out my ideas.

Tapping "Build Lineup" will open a "step-by-step" team-building feature in-place through an accordian-style animation.

Choosing players for particular positions looks as if it's step-by-step, but the position dots still function as tabs. That way, the user isn't locked-in to the feature.

The players' card design allows for more information and legibility simultaneously. More statistics can be added if necessary. Plus we can see their wonderful mugs!

Once at the final "step", the user is able to name their team and make any changes they'd like.
